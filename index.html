Welcome

We are a leading Austin based web design and creative agency providing professional website design, eCommerce solutions, and CMS systems as well as branding and graphic design services for any size business.

Our professional team is comprised of highly trained staff including a Project Manager, Graphic Designer, Web Developer, Web Designer, Programmer, Copywriter and Search Engine Optimization specialist.

We specialize in Web and eCommerce Website Design.

Over the years, we’ve been doing what we love most: conceptualizing, problem solving, designing, developing, and launching websites that our clients love. Our concept-driven site designs encompass a broad range of industries. We believe that our creative team of digital innovators has the power to impact and provide value to every industry.

Clients

We’ve worked with a diverse crowd in many different industries. We love the challenge of adapting to the unique needs and personalities of our many clients. It keeps life interesting and fresh at DOKU Studio.

Fast Turnaround

The number one question we get is, “How quickly can you have the site finished?”. We’re committed to delivering quality design and development at very fast speeds. It should take about 7 business days to see the first photoshop mockup of the design.

CSS3 & HTML 5 Coding

All sites are developed with the latest markup in CSS3 and HTML5. Websites are designed to be viewed properly across all popular browsers including Internet Explorer, Firefox, Chrome, Safari, iOS and Android devices.

Mobile Compatibility

Our sites are compatible throughout all mobile devices including iPhones, iPads and Android OS. We do not use Flash Animation in any of our designs and all slideshows, galleries and videos are viewable on all iOS devices.
